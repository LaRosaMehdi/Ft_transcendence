Game Instructions

Hello Newbies! Maybe it's your first time here.
A Stratoshperic Pong-Game will begin.
Here are the instructions for the game:

- Use the following keys to move your paddle.
Player1 (Left) will move with 'w' and 's'.
Player2 (Right) will move with 'arrowsUp' and 'arrowsDown'.

 - The objective is to hit the ball past your opponent's paddle.

 - The first player to reach 12 points wins.

 - Avoid missing the ball to prevent the opponent from scoring.

 - Both of you can put the game on Pause State with ' ' A.K.A the space-bar!

 - Have fun and good luck!
